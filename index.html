Welcome to My Quantum Tunneling Simulation

This project simulates quantum tunneling using a Schrödinger equation.

What is Quantum Tunneling?

Quantum tunneling is a phenomenon where particles pass through a potential barrier, even if they don’t have enough energy to overcome it. This happens due to the wave-like nature of particles in quantum mechanics.
